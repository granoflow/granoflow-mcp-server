# Wrapper Extension Contract

A user-owned customization must call and preserve the official `granoflow-review-card-draft` workflow. It may add policy before drafting or transform drafts before preview. It must not replace similarity search, AI prefiltering, project-task validation, shared-note impact, explicit approval, App-owned apply, or readback.

Good wrapper settings include source hierarchy and version pinning; named exam and grading conventions; split/merge rules; front/back limits; note style and language; App-supported card shapes; stricter worthiness or fact checks; and extra preview columns.

Keep defaults in the wrapper small and explicit. When the official skill gains a new capability, delegate to it rather than copying its implementation.
